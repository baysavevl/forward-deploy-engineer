STAR 01

Matching quality at Zalo scale

Situation: A recruiting product on Zalo had high-volume candidate and recruiter signals, where naive AI matching could create noisy recommendations.

Task: Apply AI in a way that preserved quality, trust, and useful lead outcomes.

Action: Researched pre-filtering, workflow design, structured explanations, and evaluation around accepted and rejected matches.

Result: Clear product and system reasoning for an 80M-user ecosystem context, directly relevant to enterprise agent deployment.

STAR 02

Recruiter onboarding support

Situation: Recruiters and partners repeated setup and troubleshooting questions, slowing onboarding and support response.

Task: Reduce friction without losing escalation quality.

Action: Built chatbot and KB-backed support patterns, source-of-truth answers, and escalation paths.

Result: Improved partner onboarding by 40% and created a reusable support-agent story.

STAR 03

Job Market workflow automation

Situation: Job Market operations required repeated manual coordination across product, sales, ops, and engineering.

Task: Make work faster and less error-prone while preserving operational ownership.

Action: Automated repeated manual actions and added AI into product and delivery workflows.

Result: Reduced operational overhead by 30% and tied engineering work to a business metric.

STAR 04

Leading a team that levels up

Situation: A four-person engineering team needed to hit delivery targets while growing professionally.

Task: Lead the team, review systems, and create conditions for promotion-level growth.

Action: Owned architecture review, stakeholder alignment, delivery discipline, and coaching.

Result: Over two promotion cycles, all team members were promoted. This is the strongest human-leadership proof in the profile.
