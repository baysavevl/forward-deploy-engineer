Technical case

AI matching at Zalo scale: quality, trust, and safe rollout.

Problem

High-volume candidate and recruiter signals can create noisy AI matches if the agent starts with generation.

System move

Filter first, explain only promising matches, keep outreach behind human review, then learn from accepted and rejected leads.

Why it matters

The solution protects recruiter trust while making matching quality measurable enough to improve.

Architecture

From raw candidate volume to explainable recommendation

1Signal filter

Use job demand, freshness, consent, location, and eligibility before calling expensive model steps.

2Rank and explain

Generate match reasons only for promising candidates, with source citations and recruiter-facing explanations.

3Human gate

Recruiter approves outreach batches; uncertain or policy-sensitive matches stay in review.

4Feedback loop

Accepted, rejected, and missed leads become eval cases for quality and policy tuning.

Architecture review

Tradeoffs I would defend

Do firstRecommendation copilot before autonomous action.
WhyIt proves value while keeping consent, eligibility, and quality under human control.
ObserveUseful match rate, acceptance rate, missed leads, and rejected-reason clusters.
GraduateOnly promote actions once traces, evals, rollback, and approval behavior are stable.
